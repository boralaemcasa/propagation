Let's stretch $\pi$.

\begin{align}
  \theta \in [0, 2\pi) \Rightarrow x &= r \cos \cfrac{\theta}{r} - r \\
  y &= r \sin \cfrac{\theta}{r}\\
  \therefore (x + r)^2 + y^2 &= r^2\\
  t &\in [0, T]\\
  r(0) = 1 \,;\, r(T) \to \infty \Rightarrow r(t) &= \cfrac{T}{T - t}
\end{align}

Glue up at $t = T$ the segment $OA$, where $A = (0, \pi)$.
